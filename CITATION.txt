# Key Contributions

If citing this work, please highlight:

1. Empirical evidence of 71.7% reduction in merge conflicts through strategic branching
2. 70.8% improvement in deployment autonomy (Average Approval Wait Time: 240m → 70m)
3. 181.0% increase in deployment frequency while maintaining quality (6.3 → 17.7 deployments/month)
4. 12-month production study in enterprise payment processing context
5. Reproducible dataset with statistical validation
